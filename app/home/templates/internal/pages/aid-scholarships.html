Scholarship Tickets

Financial barriers shouldn’t prevent participation. Scholarship Tickets provide FREE conference tickets for eligible applicants.

These scholarships are supported by our generous patrons and are available to students, teachers, and researchers. Note: Airfare and accommodation are not included.

Who can apply?

Anyone with a valid organization or company ID who requires financial support.

Scholarship Tickets Deadline

February 8, 2026, at 11:59 PM PH time

Scholarship Tickets Notification Timeline

Applicants will be notified via email or SMS by the 3rd week of February 2026.
