Partnership

Partner With Us

At Python Philippines (PythonPH), we're more than a community; we're a force for innovation and collaboration in the tech world, actively driving positive change. For PythonAsia 2026, we seek partnerships with non-profits dedicated to advancing Python and leveraging technology for social good. Together, we aim to foster a flourishing Python community and drive technological progress in the Philippines and Asia.

Why Partner With Us?

Partnering with PythonPH offers the chance to be part of a transformative journey. It's an opportunity to align with a community dedicated to Python development and to contribute meaningfully to the professional and personal growth of tech enthusiasts across the Philippines and Asia.

Our Ideal Partner

We are looking for non-profit organizations and community-focused entities that:

  • Align with our mission of open-source development and community growth.
  • Directly contribute to or support the Python ecosystem.
  • Are committed to promoting diversity and inclusivity in technology.
  • Uphold ethical and non-predatory practices, firmly standing against exploitative marketing, unfair labor practices, and other unethical activities.
  • Foster grassroots, community-driven initiatives.

Note for For-Profit Organizations

For-profit entities interested in supporting PythonAsia 2026 are encouraged to consider sponsorship opportunities.

Get in Touch

If your organization shares our vision for a vibrant Python community, we would love to explore partnership possibilities with you. Together, we can make PythonAsia 2026 a landmark event, driving innovation and community development.

Contact Us

To discuss partnership opportunities, please reach out to us at marketing@python.ph. We look forward to building a strong, collaborative future together at PythonAsia 2026.
