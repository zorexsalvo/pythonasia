PythonAsia 2026 in Manila

Everything you need to know about the venue, transport, accommodations, and local eats.

📍 Venue

De La Salle University (DLSU)
2401 Taft Avenue, Malate, Manila, 1004 Metro Manila

All talks, workshops, and sprints will be held inside the DLSU campus. The venue is easily reachable via LRT-1 (Vito Cruz Station).

🏫 Venue Facilities Guide

  • Entrance Gate: Gate 2 (North Gate)
  • Main Hall: Teresa Yuchengco Auditorium (7th–9th Flr., Bldg. 10)
  • Secondary Hall: Verdure Multi-Purpose Room (4th Flr.)
  • Sponsor Area: Verdure Lobby (4th Flr. Function Lobby)
  • Dining & Prayer Room: Pardo Hall Multi-Purpose Room (5th Flr.)
  • Breakout / Workshop Rooms: TBA
  • Poster Session & Networking Area: TBA

Muslim prayer space is available in a designated breakout room within the Dining Hall. Visit the Help Desk for directions or assistance.

🗒️ Road Awareness Tips

Traffic behavior may differ from what you’re used to. Pedestrian rules are not always followed.

  • Expect heavy traffic: 7–10 AM and 4–8 PM
  • Use pedestrian lanes and wait for vehicles to fully stop
  • Keep belongings secure in busy areas

🚗 Transportation

From NAIA: Public transit routes are available online.
From Taft: LRT-1 → Vito Cruz Station

Ride-hailing (recommended): Grab, Green GSM, InDrive (cars); Angkas, Move It, JoyRide (motorcycles)

Taxis may attempt unmetered fares. Prefer app-based rides for transparent pricing.
