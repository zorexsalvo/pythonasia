Important for all attendees

Event Reminders

As we get closer to the event, here are a few important reminders to help you prepare.

What to Bring

  • Laptop (especially for workshops and sprints)
  • Reusable water bottle
  • Your ticket QR code ready for scanning

Reminder:
Pet bottles and plastic cups are not allowed inside the premises of De La Salle University Manila.

Registration

  • Registration starts at 8:00 AM and ends at 2:00 PM.
  • Please prepare your ticket QR code for faster entry.
  • You may be asked to confirm data privacy consent during registration.

Dress Code

Please follow the university guidelines of De La Salle University Manila.

  • Revealing clothes are not allowed.
  • We recommend wearing comfortable attire suitable for a conference setting.

Conference Gate Access

  • Use the designated conference gate for attendees.
  • Follow the event signage and volunteer instructions upon arrival.

Parking Reminder

  • There are no parking spaces available inside De La Salle University Manila.
  • Attendees are encouraged to park at nearby public parking areas.

Venue Guide

A venue and university map guide will be provided to help you navigate the campus and find the conference halls easily.

See you all this March 21–23! 🎉
